My Story

I am a senior at Seven Lakes High School and I intend to major in Math/CS. I am the first author of a cybersecurity research paper published in IEEE. I'm also experienced in competitive programming having reached the Gold Division of the USA Cmoputing Olympiad. I'm involved in many clubs and organizations like Science Olympiad, National Science Bowl, Mu Alpha Theta, and Varsity Orchestra. I have shared my knowledge of science on Udemy, where I have created an astrophysics course and a public health course. I am interested in exploring the intersections between Mathematics, Computer Science, Astrophysics, Data Science, and Machine Learning, and I am open to opportunities for the same.
